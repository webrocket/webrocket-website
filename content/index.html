We believe that real-time applications are future of the Web. We want developers to have reliable, convenient and open solution to write real-time websites with no fuss.
We connected the most promissing web technology - WebSockets, with the best concurrency solution - Messaging. We are sure it is going to bring a fresh, bidirectional spirit to your apps. So here it is, our small step towards bidirectional, real-time Web!
Fast and scalable, distributed WebSockets broker · Well documented, powerfull and easy to use · Supporting many programming languages · Open source or cloud hosted version · With commercial support and trainings.

How it works?

  • Browsers

    All browsers supporting WebSockets or Flash can connect to WebRocket, subscribe channels, listen for and broadcast events or trigger backend operations.

  • Clients

    Backend clients can deal with authentication or manage channels and broadcast events to them.

  • Workers

    Backend workers can connect to WebRocket and listen for the triggered operations.

  • Broker

    WebRocket ties everything up - broadcasts data from the backend clients or browsers to all others on the channel, passes trigger requests to the workers and provides full and flexible management API.
